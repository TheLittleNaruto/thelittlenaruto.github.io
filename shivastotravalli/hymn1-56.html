Shivastotravalli 1.5:

जयन्ति भक्तिपीययूषरसासववरोन्मदाः। अद्वितीया अपि सदा त्वद्द्वितीया अपि प्रभोः।।

"O Ishwar! We hail those great sages who stay drunk by taking the wine of the nectar of your devotion and because of that they receive your abode even if they are living in this worldly life."

Shivastotravalli 1.6:

अनन्तानन्दसिन्धोस्ते नाथ तत्त्वों विदन्ति ते। तादृशा एव ये सान्द्रभक्तयानन्दरसाप्लुताः।।

"O Lord! Only those devotee can know the secret of the tattva of your infinite bliss whose heart stays in the joy of your devotion always."

Commentaries:

Mind Dissolved ji: Saints, realized ones are the one who are doped and occupied with My Lord My God SHIVA. Such saints enjoy each and every moment in the divine association of my Lord my God SHIVA even after staying in world. Only such divine Atman knows that my lords my God SHIVA's grace is open secret source of infinite bliss, they love to be in this state of love and devotion.
